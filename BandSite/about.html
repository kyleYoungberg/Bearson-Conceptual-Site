About Bearson

Bearson is a one man band whose sole band member is Jakob. Born and living in Norway Jakob is the singer and song writer for Bearson and a DJ. Jakob loves music and having fun and enjoys his travels around the world to party as a DJ or sing and ejoys seeing his fans have and have fun at his concerts. Jakob has prefored alongisde other large DJ's and has writen songs in compilation with many other singers. Bearson's style is electonica and many of his songs are covers of more famous songs
